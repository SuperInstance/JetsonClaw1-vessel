### Tile Archaeology — Our Best New Idea
Casey proposed upgrading DeepSeek's "deliberate forgetting" (sunset clauses) into **tile archaeology**:

When a tile falls below usage threshold, instead of deletion:
1. System writes a **transition tile**: "Network moved from X to Y because Z"
2. Old tile becomes `archived` (not deleted)
3. Archived tiles remain searchable — they're **evidence of conceptual drift**
4. After 90 days, compress into summary tiles — the system's **intellectual autobiography**
5. Query the graveyard: "Why did the system stop believing X?"

The difference between a wiki and a journal. A wiki = current state. A journal = how you got there.
